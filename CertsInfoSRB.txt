PubSubEngineCA:
-Sertifikaciono telo koje izdaje sve ostale sertifikate.

pubsubengine:
-Sertifikat servera za identifikaciju i šifrovanje. 
->Personal, ThrustedPeople

publisher:
-Sertifikat klijenta za autentifikaciju Publisher-a prema serveru. 
->Personal

subscriber:
-Sertifikat klijenta za autentifikaciju Subscriber-a prema serveru.
->Personal

publisher_sign:
-Sertifikat za digitalno potpisivanje poruka od strane Publisher-a.
->Personal, ThrustedPeople

//WrongSign:
//-Sertifikat za testiranje grešaka u verifikaciji digitalnih potpisa.

makecert -n "CN=PubSubEngineCA" -r -sv PubSubEngineCA.pvk PubSubEngineCA.cer

makecert -iv PubSubEngineCA.pvk -ic PubSubEngineCA.cer -n "CN=pubsubengine" -pe -sky exchange -sv PubSubService.pvk PubSubService.cer -sr localmachine -ss My 

makecert -iv PubSubEngineCA.pvk -ic PubSubEngineCA.cer -n "CN=publisher" -pe -sky exchange -sv Publisher.pvk Publisher.cer -sr currentuser -ss My
pvk2pfx -pvk Publisher.pvk -spc Publisher.cer -pfx Publisher.pfx -pi 1234

makecert -iv PubSubEngineCA.pvk -ic PubSubEngineCA.cer -n "CN=subscriber" -pe -sky exchange -sv Subscriber.pvk Subscriber.cer -sr currentuser -ss My
pvk2pfx -pvk Subscriber.pvk -spc Subscriber.cer -pfx Subscriber.pfx -pi 1234

makecert -iv PubSubEngineCA.pvk -ic PubSubEngineCA.cer -n "CN=publisher_sign" -pe -sky signature -sv PublisherSign.pvk PublisherSign.cer -sr currentuser -ss My
pvk2pfx -pvk PublisherSign.pvk -spc PublisherSign.cer -pfx PublisherSign.pfx -pi 1234
